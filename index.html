Frequently Asks Questions

  • Dog sitting is a service where individuals care for dogs in their own home or the owner's home while the owner is away. The sitter provides feeding, walking, and companionship for the dog

  • Dog sitting involves finding a suitable sitter, arranging care details, dropping off the dog, receiving updates, and picking up the dog afterward

  • To choose the right dog sitter, consider experience, compatibility, communication, safety measures, trustworthiness, and arrange a meet-and-greet before booking.

Advantages of dog sitting include personalized care, comfort, maintenance of routines, socialization opportunities, and additional services compared to dog kennels
